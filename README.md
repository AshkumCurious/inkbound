# Inkbound 📖✨

**AI-Powered Interactive Fiction**  
*Where every choice shapes the story — and every word teaches you something.*

---

## ✨ Features

- **5 Immersive Story Themes**
  - Love • Adventure • Creepy • Mystery • Sci-Fi

- **True Interactive Storytelling**
  - AI-generated stories powered by **GPT-4o**
  - Branching narratives based on your decisions
  - Custom story starter (up to 100 characters)

- **Smart Reading Experience**
  - **Tap any word** → Instant dictionary definition
  - Save words to your **Personal Vocabulary**
  - Beautiful mood-adaptive animated Story Reader character

- **New: Comprehension Quizzes** 🎯
  - Automatic short MCQ quiz after every 3 story segments
  - Tests your understanding of the story
  - Score tracking with explanations
  - Enhances retention and engagement

- **Premium Aesthetic**
  - Dark academia + magical realism design
  - Rich typography (Playfair Display & Space Mono)
  - Smooth animations and glassmorphism

Demo:::::
"Hi everyone,
I want to tell you about something I built called Inkbound.
Imagine reading a story… but it’s not just a story you read.
It’s a story that changes with every choice you make.
A story where every single word can teach you something new.
That’s Inkbound.
The Idea
Most reading apps give you content.
Most story apps give you choices.
But I wanted to create something that does both — beautifully.
Inkbound is an AI-powered interactive fiction app where:

You choose a world — Romance, Adventure, Horror, Mystery, or Sci-Fi.
You start the story… or write your own beginning in your own words.
As you read, you can tap on any word you don’t fully know.
You get its meaning instantly, and you can save it to your personal vocabulary.
After every few chapters, the app gently tests you with a short quiz — not to judge you, but to help you remember and understand the story better.

Why I Built This
I love reading.
But I noticed two problems:

We forget a lot of beautiful words we read.
Most interactive stories feel like games, not like literature.

I wanted to create an experience that feels like a real book — emotional, atmospheric, and literary — but smarter and more personal.
Inkbound is for people who love stories and also want to grow while enjoying them.
It’s for book lovers, language learners, writers, and dreamers.
Special Touches

The character who reads the story with you actually changes expression and mood according to the theme.
The design feels warm, literary, and magical — like sitting in an old library with glowing candles.
Every story feels unique because the AI remembers your choices.


Short Version (for quick intro):
"Inkbound is an interactive story app where you don’t just read — you live the story.
You make choices that change the plot, tap any word to learn its meaning, save it to your vocabulary, and even get fun quizzes to check how well you’re understanding the story.
It’s made for people who want to enjoy great stories while quietly improving their English and vocabulary at the same time."
